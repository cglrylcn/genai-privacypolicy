Introduction

CYApps ("we", "our", "us") operates the GenAI - Image Generator mobile application (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it may be shared, including with third-party AI service providers. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. User-Provided Content

When you use the AI image generation features, we collect:

  • Text prompts you enter
  • Images you manually select from your device's photo library

This data is necessary to generate AI-based images.

2. Device & Usage Data

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage data (interactions, crashes, diagnostics)

How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide AI image generation functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Monitor usage and detect issues
  • Enhance user experience

AI Processing & Data Sharing

To provide core functionality, user content is transmitted to a third-party AI service provider. This section explains exactly what data is shared, with whom, and how your consent is obtained.

What Data is Shared

  • User-entered text prompts
  • Images manually selected by the user from their device's photo library

How Data is Collected

Images are collected only when the user manually selects a photo from their device's photo library within the app. No images are accessed or collected automatically or in the background.

Who We Share Data With

We use Stability AI to process image-to-image transformation requests. Stability AI acts solely as a data processor and handles submitted content only to generate the outputs requested by the user. Stability AI maintains privacy and data protection standards equivalent to those described in this policy. You can review their privacy policy at: https://stability.ai/privacy-policy

Purpose of Data Sharing

Data is shared strictly for AI image generation and transformation. We do not use this data for advertising or any other purpose.

User Consent

Before any image or personal data is transmitted to Stability AI, the app displays a consent screen that clearly identifies:

  • What data will be sent (the user-selected photo)
  • Who it will be sent to (Stability AI)
  • The purpose of transmission (image-to-image transformation)

The user must explicitly tap "Accept" to proceed. If the user declines, no data is transmitted to any third party.

Data Retention

Images transmitted to Stability AI are used solely to complete the requested processing and are not stored by CYApps beyond the duration of the request. Please refer to Stability AI's privacy policy for their retention practices.

Third-Party Services

We may also use the following services for app optimization and advertising:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — Used for app performance monitoring and crash reporting. Collects anonymous usage and diagnostic data.
  • Google AdMob — Used for in-app advertising. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

User Rights & Security

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, delete, or correct your data. We protect your information using HTTPS/TLS encryption and secure cloud infrastructure.

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations including GDPR and CCPA.
